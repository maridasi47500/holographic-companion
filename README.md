☕🌍  Time-Hopping Travel & Cafe Experience App

This international app blends time, travel, and taste into one immersive experience:

    City & Cafe Hopper: Explore cities through curated trails and hop between cafes and bars.

    Time-Based Naming: Cafes or bars are named based on when drinks are served—using natural language like “2 hours ago” or “last Tuesday.”

    Custom Coffee Moments: Choose your visit time and duration, then personalize your coffee experience.

    Seasonal Sports Explorer: Discover sports tied to seasons across different cities—become a time-traveling sports enthusiast.

    Holographic Companions: Journey with a hologram of your favorite singer as your travel buddy.
